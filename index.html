An interactive application, both fun and educational.

HTTP Adventure Game is a platform for students to learn the HTTP Protocol while playing through a real game.

For each main scene, the user will overcome obstacles (monsters, events, etc.) which will engage him with trivia-type questions and also clues (possible false). And there'll also be rewards!

Why is learning through games important?

Gaming is an environment that encourages interactivity and dynamism. A good question-type game helps the player to master the notion on which the game is built.

Games excel at rewarding the productive struggle, the kind of struggle that is engaging and motivating, not tedious.

Games reward the productive struggle.

Studies suggest gaming is good for developing intelligence.

When game-based learning systems are compared against conventional assessment methods, the difference is clear: games are better at helping students retain knowledge, concentrate and perform at a higher level of achievement.
